Call for Papers (TBD)

We invite submissions on all aspects of data-centric AI including but not limited to:

  • Data quality, preprocessing, labeling, and governance
  • Bias detection, correction, and fairness in datasets
  • Low-resource learning, weak supervision, and semi-supervised techniques
  • Tools for dataset versioning, visualization, and audit

Submission Details (TBD)

We invite the submission of regular research papers (6-10 pages), including the bibliography and any possible appendices. Submissions must be in PDF format, and formatted according to IEEE Conference Template. Submitted papers will be assessed based on their novelty, technical quality, potential impact, insightfulness, depth, clarity, and reproducibility. All the papers are required to be submitted via the TBD. By the unique ICDM tradition, all accepted workshop papers will be published in the dedicated ICDMW proceedings published by the IEEE Computer Society Press. For more questions about the workshop and submissions, please send email to pfwang@cnic.cn.
